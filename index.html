How I Work

01 / Approach

Systems before screens

I design the model before the interface. Every screen is a consequence of decisions made upstream — about structure, hierarchy, and how the product evolves over time.

02 / Craft

Clarity is craft

The best design is the one that disappears. I obsess over reducing cognitive load — not because it looks clean, but because it earns trust. Complexity is a design failure wearing a feature hat.

03 / Collaboration

Design is a conversation

I work with engineers, PMs, and data teams early and often. The best design decisions I've made happened in a whiteboard session, not Figma. Influence matters more than ownership.

About
Based in — Berlin, Germany Available — Globally / Remote Focused on — B2B SaaS & Platforms Strategy + Craft

I've spent 10+ years designing B2B SaaS products where design decisions directly impact adoption, revenue, and strategic risk.

I work best at the intersection of product strategy and craft. I partner closely with Product and Engineering to frame ambiguous problems, surface early signals, and help teams make confident decisions — before over-investing.

My work has influenced roadmap direction, improved deal win rates, and accelerated delivery through scalable design systems. I'm hands-on by default, and most effective when design is treated as a business partner — not a service function.
