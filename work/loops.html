Picking app:
Designing for speed

Warehouse pickers navigate a handheld device for hours a day — routing, scanning, confirming. Every second of friction compounds across thousands of picks a shift. I designed the Picking app from zero to launch: faster routing, fewer taps, clearer feedback, and a UI built for glance-and-go use in a loud, physical environment.

The Picking app is a native mobile tool for warehouse operatives working in high-volume fulfilment centres. Workers scan hundreds of items per shift through dense product catalogues under constant time pressure. The previous tool was a desktop admin panel ported to mobile — every interaction designed for someone sitting at a desk, not moving at pace across a warehouse floor. The brief was simple: close that gap.
